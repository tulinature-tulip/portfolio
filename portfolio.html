Accessible Storytelling

The Journey of Martha to Planet Lyra

A psychological-emotional children's book written in honor of my daughter Martha. Each chapter is a metaphor for one of her inner worlds: diving into the ocean represents her inner storms; meeting the fire dragon represents her anger - and instead of drawing a sword, Martha finds a regulated, compassionate way to touch the dragon's heart. Between chapters, open-ended questions encourage meaningful dialogue between parent, therapist, and child. Written for all children, and especially suited for children with special needs.

Educator: "A wonderful tool for developing awareness of the life journey we all go through"

About

The person behind the work

I'm Talia - a single mother, creative professional, and someone who is genuinely curious and keeps learning every day, based in Kfar Saba. My background spans psychology, operations, fundraising, and creative content. What connects all of it is one skill: making complex things human and impactful.

My daughter Martha, who inspired the De La VegArt brand and my children's book, taught me to see the world through eyes that need stories to be told differently - and that changed how I create everything.

I'm looking for an organization that believes content and storytelling are connection, that accessibility is a value and not an afterthought, and that wants someone who will stay, grow, and build something meaningful together.
